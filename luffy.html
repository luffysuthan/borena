Why Choose Borena?

Our banana fiber tableware combines sustainability with exceptional performance, offering an eco-friendly alternative that stands up to daily use.

100% Biodegradable

Our products decompose naturally within 60-90 days, returning to the earth without leaving microplastics or toxic residues. Unlike conventional plastics that persist for centuries, our tableware completes its lifecycle responsibly.

Chemical-Free & Safe

Made purely from banana sheath fibers with no synthetic additives, bleaches, or harmful coatings. Safe for all ages and perfect for sensitive users who prefer natural materials.

Heat & Leak Resistant

Engineered to withstand temperatures up to 100°C, making them ideal for hot beverages and meals. The natural fibers create a tight weave that prevents leaks and spills.

Our Story

Who We Are

Borena is more than just a name—it's a symbol of life, renewal, and purpose. Rooted in the rich linguistic and cultural heritage of both Slavic and African traditions, Borena is believed to mean “to bring life” or “to flourish.” In the Sesotho language, it signifies royalty and sovereignty—a reflection of our commitment to lead with integrity, innovation, and environmental stewardship.

At Borena, we craft eco-friendly tea cups made from banana sheaths—a natural, biodegradable material that honors the Earth and reduces waste. Our products are a blend of tradition and innovation, designed for modern lifestyles yet deeply connected to nature.

We believe in sustainability not as a trend, but as a way of life. Every Borena tea cup embodies simplicity, purity, and craftsmanship. Our mission is to inspire a return to conscious living—where every sip is a reminder that we can flourish while preserving the planet.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To become the global benchmark for sustainable tableware, proving that eco-friendly alternatives can be both beautiful and practical while creating positive social impact.

Our Mission:

  • Environmental: Replace 1 billion plastic tableware items annually by 2030 through scalable, natural solutions
  • Social: Create dignified employment for 500 rural artisans by 2025, with a focus on women's empowerment
  • Innovation: Continuously improve our products through R&D while maintaining our chemical-free promise
  • Education: Raise awareness about sustainable living through every product we sell

Our Sustainability Impact

True sustainability goes beyond the product - it's about our entire process and the positive ripple effects we create

Circular Economy Model

We've created a closed-loop system where banana sheath - traditionally burned as waste - becomes valuable raw material, reducing agricultural pollution while creating eco-products

Low-Impact Manufacturing

Our production uses 80% less water than paper pulp processing and runs on solar energy. All byproducts are composted or recycled - we achieve near zero waste

Plastic-Free Promise

From raw materials to packaging, we eliminate plastics at every stage. Even our shipping materials are plant-based and compostable

Farmer Partnerships

We pay premium prices for banana sheath, providing farmers with 15-20% additional annual income from what was previously waste

End-of-Life Solution

Our products compost in 60-90 days in home compost systems, completing the natural cycle. We provide composting guides with every order

Community Development

5% of profits fund rural education initiatives, including environmental awareness programs in local schools

Our Craftsmanship Process

Traditional techniques meet modern sustainability in our 8-step manufacturing process

1. Ethical Sourcing

We collect banana sheaths from partner farms within 50km radius, ensuring fresh materials and reducing transport emissions

2. Natural Preparation

Sheaths are washed in rainwater and sun-dried - no chemicals or artificial drying methods are used

3. Precision Cutting

Artisans hand-cut the prepared fibers to optimal sizes for different products, minimizing waste

4. Eco-Molding

Fibers are pressed into shape using our solar-powered hydraulic machines at low temperatures

5. Natural Curing

Items dry naturally in shaded areas for 48 hours to achieve perfect durability

6. Hand Finishing

Each piece is smoothed and inspected by our quality team - no two pieces are exactly alike

7. Rigorous Testing

We test for heat resistance, leakproofness, and durability before approval

8. Sustainable Packaging

Products are packed in recycled paper with plant-based inks, ready for eco-conscious use
